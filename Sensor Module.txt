Sensor Module:
Handles pulse and blood pressure sensor initialization and readings.
Ensures sensor health and provides raw data for further processing.
Display Module:
Manages the OLED display updates.
Ensures minimal latency for real-time updates.
Control Module:
Controls vehicle integration (e.g., car stop/start) and alarms.
Handles debouncing and state management for critical conditions.
Logger Module:
Logs all health readings and alerts with timestamps.
Provides a debugging interface for developers.
